About Me

I am Dr Sanjaya Sapkota, an MBBS doctor with a passion for critical‑care medicine. Currently, I serve in the Intensive Care Unit (ICU), providing life‑saving care to patients in their most vulnerable moments.

With extensive training in emergency medicine and critical care, I specialize in managing complex cases that require immediate intervention and continuous monitoring. My approach combines evidence‑based practice with compassionate care.

Beyond treating patients, my mission is to educate the public about health and wellness. Through my blog and community outreach I share accurate medical information that empowers people to make informed decisions about their health.
